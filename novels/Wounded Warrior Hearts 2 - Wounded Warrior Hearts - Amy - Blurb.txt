Army nurse Amy Stuart has more scars than she can handle...

Amy returns home from a field hospital explosion with scars on her face and two missing fingers. Her boyfriend tells her she doesn’t meet his requirements any longer. Then rejection after rejection on job interviews leaves her with no reason to keep fighting.

Dr. Blake Lloyd collides with a tearful Amy as she flees a hospital interview. He sees the caring woman beneath the scars.

Can Blake give Amy a reason to live and heal the invisible scars as well as help her see the visible ones aren’t important?
